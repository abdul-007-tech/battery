Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which battery to buy?
You can search by your phone model on our website. If you're unsure, contact our support team with your phone details and we'll help you find the right battery.
Are your batteries genuine?
Yes, all our batteries are 100% genuine and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. We provide a 1-year warranty on all products.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for defective or incorrect items. The battery must be unused and in its original packaging. Please contact us before returning any items.
How long does delivery take?
Most orders are delivered within 2-3 business days via our partnered courier services. Express delivery options are available at checkout for faster shipping.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our services to other countries in the near future.
